Cross device tracking technology

Individual targeting

We built a cross device tracking technology from scratch using e-commerce website data. After exploratory analysis, we designed and developed a custom algorithm able to identify unique recurring cross-device visitors on a website with high accuracy. The delivery and implementation of our solution lead to a succesful aquisition of our client, Tactads, by Mediamath.

Sales effectiveness

Sales success algorithm

Performing analysis over Salesforce©, email and calendar data, we identified key success factors of a sales process for most industries. We developed a forecasting algorithm and our research oriented us toward building for our client and partner Chili Piper a smart alerts engine and smart virtual assistant that allow salespeople to take the right action at the right time, as well as a workload and pipeline optimizer.

Art market prediction

Financial algorithm and recommendation engine

We ran analyses and performed modelling over art market data in order to help anticipate artwork and artist value changes on the market, detect early success and predict price evolution of traded works. Our model serves as the basis of investment and trading strategies

Sport prediction

Predictive algorithms and recommendation engines

We are developing advanced models for prediction in several professional sports, including tennis and basketball. The data we process and analyse include numeric, semantic and unstructured data, to provide insights on competitions output, players performance or strategy. Our technology and research include modelling, statistical analysis, NLP (natural language processing) and machine learning techniques.
