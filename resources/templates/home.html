We help transform your data into value



We help companies define their data strategy, we designs tailored algorithms to optimise the performance of their digital assets, and accompany their teams towards data literacy for a complete data transformation.







Consulting



Value Assessment

We perform exploratory analysis of your data coupled with a thorough audit of data capabilities and data-related business opportunities to identify and evaluate the potential value of future data projects.

Data Strategy

Based on our value assessment and the business goals and constraints, we articulate recommendations as how to conduct the data transformation of our clients, whether globally or in a sub-part of their activity.

Data Management

We provide expertise and advice on which DMP architecture and big data technologies should be implemented to optimise the efficiency, robustness and reliability of the company's data.

Algorithm Design

Transformation



Training

Our "data science for managers" training brings data literacy to managers and teams, a strong prerequisite to successfully integrate data in our clients' businesses. Jalgos helps you hire and build an internal team of data scientists to deepen the data maturity of your company.
