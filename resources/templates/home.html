Data strategy

We help you define and implement relevant data technologies to boost your business

We bring together your strategic business goals with an analysis of your workflows, data requirements, and technical capabilities. We provide a development roadmap to achieve short-term benefit and prepare long-term innovation.

In short:

  • Diagnostic of your current data capabilities
    • We perform a thorough analysis of your current data architecture, data management platform, technologies and capabilities.
  • Identification of new data opportunities and requirements
    • Working with you to connect your business objective to data requirement, we will identify and articulate data requirements and technical needs.
  • Definition of a priotized roadmap to achieve success
    • We identify a path to create or enhance your data-driven products, and achieve your big data transition.
  • Guidance towards fluency in data related technologies
    • Through training and formations, we make sure your teams get familiar with or even fluent in data technologies and data-based reasoning, to make them effective advocates of your data strategy.
  • Decision analysis
    • We provide strategic and business data-backed consulting to help you make the smart decisions. Data analysis including predictive analytics, financial pricing, reputation assessment give powerful and precious insights for business decisions.
  • Technology and architecture advisory
    • We stay up to date on the latest innovations in big data, data science, machine learning and artificial intelligence. We provide advisory services for architecture and technology choices, as well as build vs. buy recommandations.

Algorithm design

Custom solution development and prototyping that perfectly fit your goals

For most of data-related business situations, no general purpose solution is able to provide satisfactory result or any result at all without previous modelling and insight discovery. This is when custom prototyping and algorithm design is necessary.

Usual steps

Even if each situation is different, the methodology to solve custom problems usually involves most of the following steps

  • Data discovery
    • Data discovery, gathering and cleansing set the basis of all following analyses and development and are crucial for the success of the mission.
  • Insight discovery
    • Exploratory analysis coupled with business understanding allows to verify or challenge known insights as well as discovering new directions.
  • Mathematical modelling and algorithm design
    • The most challenging step consists of designing the best algorithm given all available mathematical and technical tools based on previous insight discovery and real world understanding of the goals. Our solutions give the best answer to your goals and machine learning allow them to dynamically adapt to changes.
  • Prototyping and implementation
    • Once the research is done and the machine learning algorithm design is defined, we bring the tailored solution directly in your system as a "plug and play" tool that will provide real time results in the form of automated actions, reporting, visualization.
  • Custom visualization
    • If needed, we provide you with state of the art visualization and reporting on top of your custom solution.
  • Team guidance
    • Finally, we can help your team get familiar with the technology provided through training and guidance. We also provide maintenance on demand.
